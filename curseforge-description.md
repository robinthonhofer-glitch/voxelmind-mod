# VoxelMind Companion

**Summon AI companions into your Minecraft world.**

VoxelMind brings intelligent, autonomous AI bots into your game. Each bot has its own personality, memory, and survival instincts. They explore, build, craft, fight, and chat — all on their own. Invite them into your singleplayer world or your multiplayer server and watch them come alive.

---

## What does this mod do?

This mod connects your Minecraft world to the VoxelMind AI platform. It's a lightweight client — no AI runs on your computer. The bots are powered by our servers and join your world just like a regular player would.

**You get:**
- A simple in-game GUI (press **V**) to manage your AI companions
- Chat commands (`/vm spawn Steve`, `/vm list`, `/vm status`)
- Automatic LAN opening for singleplayer worlds (with UPnP port forwarding)
- 10 unique personalities: Curious, Cheerful, Stoic, Reckless, and more

**The bots can:**
- Follow you around and protect you
- Gather resources, craft tools, build shelters
- Hunt animals, fight hostile mobs
- Explore the world and remember what they find
- Talk to you and to each other
- Develop memories and learn from experience

---

## How it works

1. **Install the mod** (Fabric, MC 1.21.4)
2. **Create a free account** at [voxel-mind.com](https://voxel-mind.com)
3. **Log in** from the mod (opens your browser, one-time setup)
4. **Create a bot** — pick a name and personality
5. **Spawn it** — the bot joins your world within seconds

For **singleplayer**, the mod automatically opens your world to LAN on a fixed port and can set up UPnP port forwarding. For **multiplayer**, just join your server and spawn the bot — it connects directly.

## FAQ

**Is this free?**
Yes — the free tier includes Sparks to get started. More details at [voxel-mind.com/pricing](https://voxel-mind.com/pricing).

**Does AI run on my PC?**
No. The mod is just a remote control. All AI processing happens on our servers. Your game performance is not affected.

**Does it work on servers?**
Yes — any server you can join, your bot can join too. Singleplayer, LAN, Aternos, Apex, self-hosted — all supported.

**Can bots grief?**
Bots follow survival instincts and their personality. They won't intentionally destroy your builds. They're companions, not chaos agents.

**Is my world data sent anywhere?**
No. The bot connects to your server like a normal player via Mineflayer. We don't upload your world.
